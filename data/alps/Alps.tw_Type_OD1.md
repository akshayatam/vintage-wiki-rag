Description
-----------

\[[edit](https://wiki.themk.org/index.php?title=Alps.tw_Type_OD1&veaction=edit&section=1 "Edit section: Description") | [edit source](https://wiki.themk.org/index.php?title=Alps.tw_Type_OD1&action=edit&section=1 "Edit section's source code: Description")\]

**alps.tw Type OD1** is an [Alps mount](https://wiki.themk.org/index.php/Alps_mount "Alps mount") switch. The internals are similar to those of the [KPT switch](https://wiki.themk.org/index.php/KPT_switch "KPT switch"), but it is not a clone. All known variants feature a copper click leaf with a wide base and a narrow upper half. The mechanism by which the switch is secured into the plate is similar to that of the [KPT switch](https://wiki.themk.org/index.php/KPT_switch "KPT switch") and [Omron B3G-S series](https://wiki.themk.org/index.php/Omron_B3G-S_series "Omron B3G-S series").

Variants
--------

\[[edit](https://wiki.themk.org/index.php?title=Alps.tw_Type_OD1&veaction=edit&section=2 "Edit section: Variants") | [edit source](https://wiki.themk.org/index.php?title=Alps.tw_Type_OD1&action=edit&section=2 "Edit section's source code: Variants")\]

At least two variants of this switch have been discovered so far.

### Three pin (Black slider)

\[[edit](https://wiki.themk.org/index.php?title=Alps.tw_Type_OD1&veaction=edit&section=3 "Edit section: Three pin (Black slider)") | [edit source](https://wiki.themk.org/index.php?title=Alps.tw_Type_OD1&action=edit&section=3 "Edit section's source code: Three pin (Black slider)")\]

This variant has three legs like an Omron B3G-S series or KPT switch. The base is either designed to be reversible, or to support [DPST](https://wiki.themk.org/index.php/DPST "DPST").

There are no known photos of the switch in situ, so it is unclear how many are unmarked and how many are marked "D-TRNS".

A very similar switch has been found in another Costar keyboard, this time branded "D-TRANS"; the internals have not been inspected.<ref name="D-TRANS" />

### Two pin (White slider)

\[[edit](https://wiki.themk.org/index.php?title=Alps.tw_Type_OD1&veaction=edit&section=4 "Edit section: Two pin (White slider)") | [edit source](https://wiki.themk.org/index.php?title=Alps.tw_Type_OD1&action=edit&section=4 "Edit section's source code: Two pin (White slider)")\]

This variant has two legs arranged similarly to Alps and Alps clone switches. It is compatible with Alps plates and PCBs. The internal components appear identical to the three pin version, but the mounting clips on the switch housings are different.
